About

There are essential details when talking about myself in general, such as particular interests and hobbies that I have. A while back, I used to be into videogames, but this love for videogames was sabotaged by the love of programming and computer science. It started during high school when learning Python fundamentals, this then snowballed into a passion for programming. When I got to college (sixth form), I experienced much more with not only different languages, but different paradigms - Object Oriented and Event-driven paradigms primarily.

Now going into university, I am very ambitious to learn new programming techniques to form the cornerstones of what will be known as my career within the IT industry.
